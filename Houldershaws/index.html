About Us

We are a family run business which was started in 1935 and has recently expanded into Sand, Gravel and Decorative Aggrigates, we are an approved Coal Merchant and a member of the Coal Merchants Federation.

Our aim is to provide a prompt, reliable and friendly service at all times.

Deliveries can be made throughout Lincolnshire (minimum quantity may apply).

Fuels are normally delivered in 50kg sacks which can be tipped straight into your Bunker or Coal House, alternatively we can supply you with 25kg pre-packed bags. The 25kg bags are also available for collection from our Depot.

If you cannot see what you are looking for please give us a ring, we are always updating our products.
